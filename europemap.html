ABOUT THE PROJECT

This map was created as a lab assignment for my introductory cartography course at UW-Madison. It was completed using Adobe Illustrator and ArcMap, and the assignment included labelling over one hundred features. This map was the first time I ever used Illustrator, and if I were to re-do it today, there are stylistic changes I would make, including putting an inner glow on the water, changing the opacity of the legend background to blend it with the land, and making the metadata less intrusive.
