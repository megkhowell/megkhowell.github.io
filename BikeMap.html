ABOUT THE PROJECT

This map was my first foray into terrain representation - in this case, shaded relief mapping. I used Terrain Sculptor, ArcMap, Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator to process the DEM and landcover, and used Google My Maps to create a fictional KML route for the bike race.
