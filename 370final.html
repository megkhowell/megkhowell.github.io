ABOUT THE PROJECT

This was my final project submission for my introductory cartography course at UW-Madison. It was completed using ArcMap and Adobe Illustrator. It examines how sex education progamming and rates of teen pregnancy vary across states, and how they could be related. I think the largest challenge of this map was determining how to represent the third variable; where the funding for sex education in each state came from. The colored borders are unobtrusive, and are at the bottom of the visual heirarchy, but allow easy comparison across states if you are looking for it.
